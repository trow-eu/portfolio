Want to know me a bit better?

Inquisitive

There is always less answers than questions. Value them, seek good answers by asking good questions. Evaluate them, learn from them, apply them.

I don't take anything for granted and approach new things like a child - with questions!
What is it? How does it work? Why was it made this way? How can I apply it?
Always analyzing things around brings many small discoveries and give many small ideas to develop into something bigger.

Creative

Learning a bit of everything is what makes my life interesting. Applying these knowledge is what makes it meaningful!

For me, being creative means what I can accept any design challenge and, regardless of relevant experience and resources, make a meaningful progress in developing the solution.
There are no impossible tasks - only lack of time and resources. But estimate if resources needed to complete the task (especially without experience) worth it.

Entrepreneur

Seeing opportunities and working hard to utilize them - that's the entrepreneurial spirit.

I'm not really a "business" type but I had some opportunities to work on interesting projects in collaboration with other people. It was either take the chance and become the entrepreneur in it or skip it. I took the chances. It was worth it.
