ICC Invaders

Outercultural communication simulator
Contribution:
Game Design, UI, Programming.
Team:
Ivian Kasabov,
Andi Vasevski,
Naduah Klencke

The goal of this project was to show players what integrating in new culture can be not that easy, but also not too hard. However our challenge was to also show what "perfect", stereotypical individuals are very unlikely and unnatural, so we created artificial individuals and gave players limited resources to programm them - imperfect like real humans.

Curious 7

Learn cultures, have fun

“Curious7” is a guessing game teaching players about cultural differences. Get one of 30 cultures, use 7 action cards to ask other players about their culture and try to guess which culture they have. It can be played by 2 to 7 players.

We gathered important feedback during playtests and improved the game so it was fun for our players and informative about other cultures.

Contribution:
Game Design, Content, Art, Print
Team:
Demi Tavares,
Lachezar Kolev,

Wings of Steam

Global Game Jam, Ukraine
It was my first participation in creating a game. I went to nearby Global Game Jam location and joined a team of 3 students. Together we created this simple top-down runner game where you control a flock of mechanical pigeons to deliver an emergency message avoiding traps and obstacles. A simple gameplay was supported by interesting and dramatic narrative set in the parallel world of Steampunk with the real-world character as the antagonist.
